## 60-second summary

I created this project to show recruiters that my experience is not limited to operating devices. I understand how controls fit together: governance, identity, network segmentation, endpoint security, vulnerability management, monitoring, incident response, and recovery. The architecture assumes breach and uses layered controls to limit compromise, detect suspicious activity, and recover safely. It is mapped to NIST CSF 2.0 and CIS Controls v8 so that technical implementation is connected to recognised security outcomes. I also positioned the design around today’s threat landscape, including supply chain attacks and major breaches affecting large organisations and public-sector entities.
